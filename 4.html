Steve Jobs

1955-2011




BIOGRAPHY
Steve Jobs (1955-2011) was a pioneering American entrepreneur and co-founder of Apple Inc. He played a key role in revolutionizing several industries, including personal computing, music, movies, and smartphones. Jobs co-founded Apple in 1976, leading the development of innovative products like the Apple II, Macintosh, iPhone, and iPad. After being ousted from Apple in 1985, he founded Next and acquired Pixar, which later became a major success. Jobs returned to Apple in 1997, transforming the company into a global tech giant. His visionary leadership and relentless drive for perfection made him one of the most influential figures in technology.
